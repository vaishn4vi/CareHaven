Your Personal Health Records

Manage and access your health information easily.

What is a Personal Health Record?

A personal health record (PHR) is a tool that helps you manage your health information. It contains your medical history, medications, allergies, and more.

Benefits of Having a PHR

  • Easy access to your health information
  • Improved communication with healthcare providers
  • Better management of your health and wellness
  • Enhanced ability to make informed decisions about your care

How to Create Your Personal Health Record

  1. Choose a PHR system that fits your needs.
  2. Gather your medical records and health information.
  3. Input your data into the PHR system.
  4. Update your record regularly.
  5. Share your information with your healthcare providers as needed.
